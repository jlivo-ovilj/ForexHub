About ForexHub
Your daily forex companion
How does the analysis work?
ForexHub sends prompts to Claude AI via a Cloudflare Worker with live web search. All 29 pairs are available across Daily and Weekly tabs.
How often should I refresh?
Daily tab: each morning AEST. Weekly tab: Monday before markets open. Calendar: once per week on Monday.
Where is my journal stored?
Your trade journal is saved in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and persists across sessions.
What session should I trade from Brisbane?
London (6pm–3am AEST) and the London/NY overlap (11pm–3am AEST) offer the highest volume for most pairs. Sydney session (8am–5pm) is best for AUD and NZD pairs.
How accurate is the position size calculator?
It uses approximate pip values per pair type. Always confirm the final lot size with your broker's own calculator before trading.
Is this financial advice?
No. ForexHub is an educational tool only. Always consult a licensed financial adviser before trading.
Recommended ASIC-regulated brokers?
Pepperstone, IC Markets, and IG Markets are popular choices for Australian forex traders.
