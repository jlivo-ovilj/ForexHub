How does the analysis work?
ForexHub sends prompts to Claude AI via a Cloudflare Worker. Analysis runs on the cheaper Haiku model and is cached per day; the scanner uses a stronger model with live prices and web search. Web search is only used where freshness matters, to keep costs down.
How do I remove the PIN gate?
The PIN is "2025". To remove it permanently for public access, delete the pin-gate div and the checkPin() / initPin() functions from the HTML source.
How often should I refresh?
Daily tab: each morning AEST. Weekly tab: Monday before markets open. 4H tab: before intraday sessions. Calendar: once per week on Monday.
Where is my journal stored?
Your trade journal is saved in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and persists across sessions.
What session should I trade from Brisbane?
London (6pm–3am AEST) and the London/NY overlap (11pm–3am AEST) offer the highest volume. Sydney session (8am–5pm) is best for AUD and NZD pairs.
Is this financial advice?
No. ForexHub is an educational tool only. Always consult a licensed financial adviser before trading.
Recommended ASIC-regulated brokers?
Pepperstone, IC Markets, and IG Markets are popular choices for Australian forex traders.
