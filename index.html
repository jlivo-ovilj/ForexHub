About ForexHub
Your daily forex companion
How does the analysis work?
ForexHub uses Claude AI with live web search to generate daily and weekly analysis based on current market conditions, price action, and economic events.
How often should I refresh?
Daily analysis is best refreshed each morning (AEST). Weekly outlook is best refreshed Monday morning before the week begins.
Is this financial advice?
No. ForexHub is an AI-powered analysis tool for educational purposes only. Always do your own research and consult a licensed financial adviser before trading.
Best pairs for beginners?
Start with EUR/USD — it has the highest liquidity, tightest spreads, and the most analysis available. Master one pair before adding others.
What broker should I use in Australia?
Look for ASIC-regulated brokers. Popular options include Pepperstone, IC Markets, and IG Markets — all regulated in Australia.
